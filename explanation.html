So... how does it work?

Every round, the player plays a random small map and chooses 2 cards that may add difficulty modifiers. These modifiers will create a custom difficulty just for this run (as of Phasmophobia's v.0.8 "Apocalypse" update), which starts as Professional difficulty and gets harder as the run goes on.

Every 3rd round, the player chooses 1 self-imposed challenge and plays a bigger map. Failing to beat a map or to complete a challenge, will fail the run.

Most cards may only be chosen once per run.


Card types

Silver cards

  The most common card type, offered on every round that is not a multiple of 3.   
These cards add modifiers to your difficulty.

 
Golden cards

They can seldomly appear alongside silver cards. Guaranteed on rounds 2, 4 & 5.
These cards change the rules of card generation and selection.

 
Challenge cards

They are offered every 3rd round.
  These cards apply a self-imposed challenge to the player, and are usually hard.
